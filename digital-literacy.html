Digital Literacy & Computer Packages

At FutureMinds, we understand that strong digital literacy is essential for learners of all ages. Our Digital Literacy & Computer Packages equip students with practical skills to confidently navigate, create, and communicate in the digital world.

What Makes It Special

  • Essential Skills: Typing, file management, email, and productivity software.
  • Safe Online Practices: Internet safety, password management, and digital etiquette.
  • Problem-Solving & Research: Learn to search effectively, evaluate sources, and create reports.
  • Practical Applications: Real-world digital projects for school, work, or personal use.

Courses & Packages Offered

Beginner Package

  • Basic computer skills and file management
  • Introduction to word processing and spreadsheets
  • Safe internet and email usage

Intermediate Package

  • Advanced productivity tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Basic presentation and project creation
  • Effective internet research and source evaluation

Advanced Package

  • Digital communication and collaboration tools
  • Creating multimedia projects (video, images, basic graphics)
  • Digital problem-solving and critical thinking exercises

Outcomes for Learners
